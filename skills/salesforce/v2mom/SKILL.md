The V2MOM should be derived from my leadership (especially Kurt Brown), but use my peers as a guide.

Vision: describes what you want to accomplish this FY. Think of the Vision as your elevator pitch and keep it to approximately 1-3 sentences. Make it bold, inspiring and focused more on the what (less on the how).

Values: the beliefs and principles that are important as you pursue your Vision. Values serve as our north star to guide us as we are delivering our work. Your values should align to the company values, however they do not need to be identical. The company values are: Trust, Customer Success, Innovation, Equality, and Sustainability.

Methods and Measures: Methods outline the actions you will take to achieve your Vision. Your Methods should be written in priority order. Measures are used to track how successful you were in delivering your Methods. Measures should be data driven and SMART — Specific, Measurable, Attainable, Relevant, Time-bound.

Obstacles: potential barriers to achieving your Vision. You can add Obstacles for your whole V2MOM.

## Guide

Do not put how the Values and Measures tie to leadership inline with the Values or Measures content, instead put how they tie to leadership in its own section.
